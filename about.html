About

Biography

R Matthews, CSM, PMP, CSM, PMP is a delivery manager at Moody's Ratings - Technology, leveraging his degrees in Computer Science and African & African American Studies a degree from Brandeis University. Since joining Moody's Analytics as a software engineer in 2019, he has demonstrated a passion for technology and people, driving impactful solutions within the organization. Alongside his professional pursuits, R finds solace in music and cherishes spending time with family and friends. While he currently resides in Charlotte, NC, his roots in Atlanta remain strong, prompting regular visits to reconnect with loved ones. You can view a copy of his résumé here.
