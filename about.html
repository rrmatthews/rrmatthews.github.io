About

Biography

R Matthews is a Delivery Manager on the Technology team at Moody's Ratings, where he leads two Agile teams and guides technology projects from first idea to launch. He does his best work at the intersection of people and product — building trust, clearing obstacles, and helping teams ship the right thing at a pace they can sustain.

R holds degrees in Computer Science (BS) and African & African American Studies (BA) from Brandeis University, and got his start at Moody's as a software engineer in 2019. In the years since, he's grown into delivery and program management, earning his Certified ScrumMaster (CSM) and Project Management Professional (PMP) credentials along the way.

Away from work, R is happiest around good music and the people he loves. He's based in Charlotte, NC, but his roots run deep in Atlanta, and he heads back often to reconnect with family and friends. You can view a copy of his résumé here.
