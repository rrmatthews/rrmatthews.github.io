Biography

I guess I should start off with my name since that's probably what's going to give people the most confusion. My name really is R Matthews. That's my first and last name. My parents were crazy enough to put that on my birth cirtificate, so here I am. Born in Brooklyn, raised in Atlanta, educated in Boston. A student at Brandeis University studying Computer Science / African & Afro-American Studies. A lover of tech, music, and people. I'm pretty cool. What else do you want to know?
