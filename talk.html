In March 2019, a team from Brandeis University's Heller School for Social Policy and Management invited me to join them in competing for the Hult Prize. They were designing an app that would let multilingual young adults serve as live interpreters over video for businesses, schools, and nonprofits, and they needed someone with a technical background to help shape the product and think through the problem ahead of the competition.
